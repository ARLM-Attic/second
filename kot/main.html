This is a loving tribute derivative second derivative of the definitive incremental Derivative Clicker. It's also my first published game.

Things you should know:

  • Ticks/second affects all cells. Ticks/click affects only the cell you're clicking.
  • Integrate weaker items to stronger ones by clicking the number next to a plus, like +3
  • When integrating, all tiers above the target tier are converted at their respective rates.
  • Use your clicks to focus development on what you think will help most, and to recover lower tiers after integration.
  • You can click anywhere on a cell, not just the title.
  • Upgrade each cell by clicking the green text near the Power listing, like 1.00M
  • Prestige will increase upgrade effectiveness, based on the highest tier you have reached in each line.
  • The game autosaves one second after each upgrade/integration, and every minute besides.
